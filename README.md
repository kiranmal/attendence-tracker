## Attendance Tracker

This Attendance Tracker makes it easy for employees to manage their daily attendance. Employees can log in using their registered mobile number, check in, check out, and keep track of their working hours throughout the day.

The application also helps employees stay on top of their daily work hours, while giving administrators a simple way to monitor attendance, manage employee records, and view reports from one place.

Only mobile numbers registered in the database are allowed to log in, ensuring secure access to the application.

**Features:**

* OTP-based secure login
* Employee check-in and check-out
* Live working hours tracking
* Attendance history
* Admin dashboard for employee management
* Attendance reports
